Privacy Policy - Prokash

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Overview

Prokash is a community incident reporting and verification platform that helps citizens report and verify real-time incidents (traffic, accidents, emergencies) from social media sources.

2. Data We Collect

  • Incident reports from social media platforms (Facebook, YouTube, Twitter)
  • Public posts and comments
  • User account information (name, profile picture)
  • Location data (when provided)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Verify incident authenticity using AI
  • Publish verified incidents to the community
  • Improve our verification algorithms
  • Provide safety alerts

4. Data Security

We encrypt sensitive data and store it securely. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
