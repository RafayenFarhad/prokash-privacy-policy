How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from Prokash, follow these steps:

Request Process

  1. Send an email to: prokashalertssystem@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include:
    • Your full name
    • Email address or account ID
    • Brief description of what data to delete
  4. Timeline: We will process your request within 30 days
  5. Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirming deletion

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account information
  • Your posts and comments
  • Your personal data from our database
  • Your activity logs

What Remains

Anonymized incident data may remain for statistical purposes, but will not be linked to your identity.

Questions?

Email: prokashalertssystem@gmail.com
